Welcome to my website!

Trained as an environmental sociologist, I have conducted research on the social and political dimensions of climate change, environmental politics, environmental justice, climate justice, climate change adaptation, health and wellbeing, and disaster risk management. I hold a Ph.D. in Sociology from the Department of Humanities and Social Sciences at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (Mumbai, India). Since 2019, I have been a faculty member in the School of Sustainability at XIM University (Odisha, India).

I have published four books, including Local Adaptation to Climate Change in South India: Challenges and the Future in the Tsunami-hit Coastal Regions (Routledge, UK), and three edited volumes focusing on environmental justice, climate justice, and socio-political perspectives on climate risks. As an academic, I have designed and delivered courses on environmental and social issues, gender, disaster management, sustainable development, environmental science, and public administration and governance.

I have received several international fellowships and travel grants over the years. I continue to network and collaborate with peers in China, the United States, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, France, Norway, South Africa, Brazil, and Kenya to strengthen my research and teaching. I am always open to discussing potential collaborations. If you are interested in collaborating with me, learning more about my work, or have any other queries, please do not hesitate to reach out to me at "devendraraj.mm@gmail.com."
