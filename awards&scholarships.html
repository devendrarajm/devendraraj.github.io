Awards & Scholarships

  • Awarded the prestigious Fulbright-Nehru Doctoral Research Fellowship (2016-2017) by the United States-India Educational Foundation (USIEF) to conduct doctoral research at the University of California, Santa Cruz, USA. (Madhanagopal, D.)
  • Received the DAAD (German Academic Exchange Service) Fellowship (2015) to attend the International Summer School on "Social Inequalities" at the University of Bremen, Germany. (Madhanagopal, D.)
  • Awarded the Erasmus Mundus NAMASTE Scholarship (2014) by the European Commission to pursue doctoral research at the University of Groningen, The Netherlands. (Madhanagopal, D.)
  • Received the ICSSR Doctoral Fellowship (2013-2015) from the Indian Council of Social Science Research (ICSSR), Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India. (Madhanagopal, D.)
  • Awarded the prestigious Institute Fellowship (2011-2016) by the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay to pursue doctoral research. (Madhanagopal, D.)
  • Received the Best Paper Award at the International Conference on "Sustainability and Climate Change" organized by the Madras School of Economics, Chennai (2019). (Madhanagopal, D.)
  • Awarded the Best Paper Award at the National Conference on "Environment and Society" organized by the Department of Humanities and Social Sciences, Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (2015). (Madhanagopal, D.)
  • Received travel grants from the International Sociological Association (ISA) to attend and present papers at the ISA World Congress of Sociology in Yokohama, Japan (2014) and Toronto, Canada (2018). (Madhanagopal, D.)
  • Awarded travel grants from the European Sociological Association (ESA) to attend and present papers at the ESA Conference in Prague, Czech Republic (2015) and Athens, Greece (2017). (Madhanagopal, D.)
  • Received the International Publication Award from XIM University, Bhubaneswar (2020, 2021, 2022). (Madhanagopal, D.)
